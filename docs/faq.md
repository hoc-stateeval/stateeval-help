# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Access

### I didn't receive my invitation email
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address is correct with your administrator
- Ask your administrator to resend the invitation

### I forgot my password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

### How do I change my email address?
Contact your district administrator to update your email address.

## For Teachers

### How do I see who my evaluator is?
Your evaluator is shown on your Dashboard and in My Evaluation.

### When should I submit artifacts?
Submit artifacts throughout the year as you create them. Don't wait until the end of the year.

### Can I edit an artifact after submitting it?
Yes, you can edit artifacts until your evaluation is finalized.

## For Evaluators

### How do I see my evaluatees?
Your assigned evaluatees appear on your Evaluator Dashboard.

### Can I reassign an evaluatee to another evaluator?
Contact your administrator to change evaluator assignments.

## For Administrators

### How do I add a new staff member?
Go to Staff Admin > All Staff > Add Staff. See [Adding Staff](./staff-management/adding-staff) for details.

### How do I send invitations to new staff?
Go to Staff Admin > Invitations and click Send for each staff member. See [Sending Invitations](./staff-management/sending-invitations).

### How do I upload a roster?
Go to Staff Admin > District Roster > Upload. See [Roster Uploads](./staff-management/roster-uploads).
