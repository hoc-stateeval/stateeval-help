# Logging In to eVAL

## First-Time Login

If you're logging in for the first time, you'll need to activate your account:

1. Check your email for an invitation from eVAL
2. Click the activation link in the email
3. Create your password
4. Log in with your email and new password

## Returning Users

1. Go to [app.stateeval.com](https://app.stateeval.com)
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Sign In**

## Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Trouble Logging In?

If you're having trouble accessing your account:

- Make sure you're using the email address your district has on file
- Check that Caps Lock is off when entering your password
- Contact your district administrator if you need your account reset
