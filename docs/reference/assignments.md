# Assignments & Plan Types

Before an evaluatee's evaluation can begin, two things must be set in the **Assignments** area:

1. an **evaluator** assigned to them, and
2. a **plan type** — Comprehensive or Focused.

Until both are in place, the evaluatee can't open their evaluation, and the assigned evaluator won't see them on their dashboard. See [Account Status & Access](../getting-started/account-status.md) for how this appears.

## Who Does This

By default, the **District Admin** manages assignments for the whole district. The district can **delegate** assignment responsibility to individual schools — in which case the School Admin, or a principal if the district has enabled principal delegation, assigns evaluators and plan types for staff at that school.

| Responsibility | Who Assigns |
|----------------|-------------|
| **District** (default) | District Admin assigns for the school |
| **School** (delegated) | School Admin or principal assigns for their school |

From the Assignments Summary page, an admin toggles delegation per school, or uses **Delegate to All Schools** to delegate all at once.

:::tip District Size
Large districts often delegate to reduce central workload; smaller districts may keep assignments centralized for consistency.
:::

## Assigning an Evaluator

Each evaluatee is assigned an evaluator based on their role:

| Evaluatee | Can Be Assigned To |
|-----------|--------------------|
| Teachers | Principal at their school, or a District Teacher Evaluator (DTE) |
| Assistant Principals | Head Principal at their school, or a District Principal Evaluator (DPE) |
| Head Principals | District Principal Evaluator (DPE) |

## Assigning a Plan Type

Each evaluatee also needs a plan type:

| Plan Type | Who's Eligible | What It Means |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Comprehensive** | Everyone | All 8 criteria are evaluated |
| **Focused** | Must have a prior Proficient/Distinguished rating | One criterion, chosen by the evaluatee |

:::tip New Staff
New teachers and principals typically start with Comprehensive evaluations.
:::

If an evaluatee's plan type changes mid-year, see [Plan Type Changes](./plan-type-changes.md).
