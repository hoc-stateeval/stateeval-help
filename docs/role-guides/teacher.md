# Teacher Guide

As a teacher in eVAL, you participate in your evaluation by submitting artifacts, completing self-assessments, and viewing feedback from your evaluator.

## Key Responsibilities

### Artifacts
- [Submitting artifacts](../artifacts/submitting-artifacts)
- Linking artifacts to evaluation criteria
- Adding context and reflections

### Self-Assessment
- Completing self-assessments
- Setting professional goals
- Reflecting on your practice

### Viewing Feedback
- Reviewing observation notes
- Viewing evaluator feedback
- Understanding your evaluation results

## Your Dashboard

Your teacher dashboard shows:
- Your assigned evaluator
- Upcoming tasks and deadlines
- Your evaluation progress

## Throughout the Year

1. **Beginning of year** - Complete self-assessment and set goals
2. **Ongoing** - Submit artifacts that demonstrate your practice
3. **Observations** - Participate in scheduled observations
4. **Mid-year** - Review mid-year feedback (if applicable)
5. **End of year** - Review final evaluation results

## Common Tasks

| Task | Where to Go |
|------|-------------|
| Submit an artifact | My Evaluation > Artifacts > Add Artifact |
| View observation notes | My Evaluation > Observations |
| See my evaluator | Dashboard or My Evaluation |
| View my evaluation | My Evaluation > Summary |
