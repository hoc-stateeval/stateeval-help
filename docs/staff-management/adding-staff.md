# Adding Staff Members

You can add individual staff members through Staff Admin.

## Steps to Add a Staff Member

1. Go to **Staff Admin** > **All Staff**
2. Click **Add Staff**
3. Fill in the required information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
4. Assign at least one role and location
5. Click **Save**

## Required Information

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| First Name | Yes | |
| Last Name | Yes | |
| Email | Yes | Must be unique in the system |
| Role | Yes | Select the staff member's role |
| Location | Yes | School or District |

## After Adding Staff

After adding a staff member:
- They will appear in the All Staff list with status "Needs Account Setup"
- Go to the **Invitations** tab to send them an activation email
- Once they activate their account, their status changes to "Active"
