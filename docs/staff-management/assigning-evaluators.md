# Assigning Evaluators

Each evaluatee needs an assigned evaluator to conduct their evaluation. Administrators assign evaluators through the roster views.

## Where to Assign Evaluators

- **District Admins**: Use **Staff Admin** > **District Roster**
- **School Admins**: Use **Staff Admin** > **School Roster**

## Assigning an Evaluator

1. Navigate to the appropriate roster view
2. Find the staff member who needs an evaluator
3. Click on their row or the assign button
4. Select an evaluator from the dropdown
5. Save the assignment

## Who Can Be an Evaluator?

The system shows only valid evaluators based on:
- The evaluatee's role (teachers vs. principals have different evaluator options)
- The evaluator's role and location
- District configuration

### For Teachers
- Head Principal at their school
- Assistant Principal at their school (if configured)
- District Teacher Evaluator (DTE)

### For Principals
- District Principal Evaluator (DPE)

## Bulk Assignment

To assign the same evaluator to multiple staff members:

1. Go to the roster view
2. Click **Bulk Assign**
3. Select the staff members
4. Choose the evaluator
5. Confirm the assignment
